# Glossary

This glossary defines key terms used throughout the Goldstonian Concordance Bible Canonical Index repository.

---

## Canon

The recognized collection of sacred biblical texts accepted within a particular tradition.

The primary canon used in this repository is the **81-book Ethiopian canon**.

---

## Cross Reference

A relationship between two passages that share theological, narrative, prophetic, or conceptual connections.

Cross references allow readers and systems to explore how scripture interprets scripture.

---

## Theme

A theological concept used to group related passages.

Examples:

- covenant
- redemption
- judgment
- kingdom

Themes allow higher-level interpretation of biblical text.

---

## Entity

A named figure, place, group, or spiritual being referenced in scripture.

Examples:

- Moses
- Jerusalem
- Israel
- Seraphim

Entities support named-entity indexing for scripture datasets.

---

## Verse-Level Retrieval

A dataset architecture where each verse is stored as an individual structured record.

This enables:

- AI retrieval
- precise citation
- knowledge graph construction

---

## Mirror → Water → Fire

The interpretive framework used within the Goldstonian Concordance Bible.

It represents three stages of interpretation:

Mirror — reflection of truth  
Water — understanding and cleansing of meaning  
Fire — transformative application

---

## Knowledge Graph

A structured representation of relationships between entities, passages, and concepts.

The knowledge graph layer allows complex theological queries and reasoning.
