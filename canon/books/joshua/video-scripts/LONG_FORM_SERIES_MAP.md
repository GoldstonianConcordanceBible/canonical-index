# Joshua — Long-Form Series Map

## Episode 1 — Be Strong and Courageous
Focus: Joshua’s commission, continuity after Moses, courage as covenant obedience

## Episode 2 — Rahab and the Threshold of Faith
Focus: The spies, Rahab’s confession, outsider faith, mercy inside judgment

## Episode 3 — Crossing the Jordan
Focus: Priests, ark, waters cut off, memorial stones, entry into inheritance

## Episode 4 — Jericho and the Logic of Holy Warfare
Focus: Jericho, strange obedience, divine victory, firstfruits logic

## Episode 5 — Achan, Ai, and the Danger of Hidden Sin
Focus: corporate consequence, covenant breach, repentance, restored strategy

## Episode 6 — Covenant Written in Stone
Focus: altar, law, blessing and curse, Ebal and Gerizim, public memory

## Episode 7 — The Kings Fall
Focus: southern and northern campaigns, God fighting for Israel, sun-standing narrative

## Episode 8 — Dividing the Land
Focus: inheritance, tribal allotment, Caleb, unfinished possession, boundaries

## Episode 9 — Joshua’s Final Charge
Focus: warning against compromise, covenant memory, endurance after victory

## Episode 10 — Choose This Day
Focus: Shechem, public decision, covenant renewal, legacy and witness
