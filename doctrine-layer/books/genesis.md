# Genesis — Doctrine Layer

**Book:** Genesis  
**Series:** Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB)  
**Doctrine:** Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine  

## Mirror
Genesis shows the reader what is true at the deepest level. God creates by word. Humanity bears divine image. Sin is revolt, not inconvenience. History is moral and covenantal. The world is broken because man broke fellowship with God.

## Water
Genesis cleanses false narratives. It corrects the lie that humanity can define itself apart from God. It restores the reader to first principles: creation, identity, family, obedience, covenant, and providence. It washes modern confusion by returning the mind to origin.

## Fire
Genesis refines through judgment and testing. The fall, the flood, Babel, barrenness, exile, famine, and betrayal all become instruments that purify the covenant line. Fire in Genesis is not random pain; it is divine formation.

## Goldstonian Reading
Within the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB), Genesis is the canonical ignition point. It should be read not merely as ancient narrative but as the theological source code of Scripture. Every later doctrine, covenant, kingdom pattern, priestly tension, and messianic development grows from seeds planted in Genesis.
