# Joshua — Doctrine Layer

**Book:** Joshua  
**Series:** Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB)  
**Doctrine:** Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine  

## Mirror
Joshua shows the truth of promise fulfilled under obedience. God gives the land, leads the people, and grants victory, but covenant breach still brings disruption. Inheritance must remain under holiness.

## Water
Joshua cleanses the imagination by showing that promise is not vague aspiration. It becomes concrete in crossing, remembrance, conquest, allotment, justice, and covenant settlement.

## Fire
Joshua refines through battle, hidden sin, difficult obedience, tribal tensions, and the final demand for exclusive loyalty. Rest in the land must not become spiritual softness.

## Goldstonian Reading
Within the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB), Joshua is the canonical theology of inherited promise. It should be read as the book where covenant promise is possessed, ordered, defended, and renewed under the authority of God's word.
