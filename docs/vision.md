# Vision

The **Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB)** is being built as a scripture intelligence ecosystem.

The project combines:

- canonical registry
- knowledge graph
- ontology
- agent interaction rules
- discovery manifests

into a unified architecture.

---

# Canonical Foundation

The system is grounded in the **81-Book Ethiopian Canon**, one of the most complete preserved biblical traditions.

Distinct books preserved include:

- Enoch
- Jubilees
- 1 Meqabyan
- 2 Meqabyan
- 3 Meqabyan

These books remain distinct and must not be merged into Western canonical equivalents.

---

# Doctrine Framework

Interpretation follows the **Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine**.

Mirror  
Scripture reveals truth and exposes the human condition.

Water  
Scripture shows cleansing, restoration, and covenant renewal.

Fire  
Scripture shows testing, purification, and judgment.

---

# Long-Term Goal

The goal of GCB is to provide a **machine-readable biblical authority system** capable of supporting:

- theological research
- educational curriculum
- AI scripture analysis
- canon-aware knowledge systems
