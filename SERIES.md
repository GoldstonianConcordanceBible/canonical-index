# Series Structure

The Goldstonian Concordance Bible organizes interpretive and analytical material into thematic **Series**.

These series provide conceptual frameworks for theological analysis, commentary, and educational materials built on top of the canonical dataset.

---

## Major Series

### Series I — Foundations of Covenant and Kingdom

Focus:

- Creation
- covenant structure
- early biblical narrative
- theological foundations of scripture

---

### Series II — Wisdom and Worship

Focus:

- wisdom literature
- psalms
- prayer
- moral instruction

Books typically referenced:

- Psalms
- Proverbs
- Ecclesiastes
- Wisdom literature

---

### Series III — Prophets and the Messianic Thread

Focus:

- prophetic literature
- covenant warnings
- messianic prophecy
- Israel's national narrative

---

### Series IV — Jesus and the Church

Focus:

- the Gospels
- early church history
- apostolic teachings
- doctrinal development

---

### Series V — Eschatology and the Final Witness

Focus:

- Revelation
- apocalyptic literature
- end-time theology
- final judgment themes

---

## Purpose of the Series Layer

The Series layer allows:

- thematic indexing
- educational structuring
- theological cross-reference mapping
- curriculum generation
- commentary integration
